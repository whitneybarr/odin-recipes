Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

Make this restaurant classic at home in minutes with simple ingredients and even simpler instructions.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • ½ cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup seasoned croutons
  • ¼ cup creamy Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Arrange lettuce on four serving plates. Top with chicken, Parmesan, and croutons, then drizzle with dressing.
